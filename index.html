ABOUT

Our Mission

Nigerian social commerce moves billions of naira every year through Instagram DMs, WhatsApp groups, and TikTok storefronts. Almost none of it has any protection.

Vaultr exists to change that. Not with a workaround, but with proper infrastructure. Legal, regulated, and built to last. We are a small founding team that believes the solution to trust problems in commerce is not more warnings. It is better plumbing.

Vaultr operates with full transparency because we believe accountability is the first proof of trustworthiness. Every milestone we reach, we communicate. Every decision we make, we can defend.

If you are a vendor who has been burned by a fake payment or a buyer who has lost money to a no-show seller, this is built for you. We are not here to make money from trust. We are here to make trust cheap enough that everyone can afford it.
